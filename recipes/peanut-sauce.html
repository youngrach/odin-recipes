Peanut Sauce

Description

This quick and easy peanut sauce uses just 4 ingredients and can be adjusted to suit individual preference. It is also very easy to scale up or down and can be adapted as a salad dressing or accompaniment to finger foods.

Ingredients

  • 102 grams peanut butter
  • 39 grams light soy sauce
  • 45 grams lemon juice
  • Water to thin

Steps

  1. Combine the peanut butter, soy sauce, and lemon juice.
  2. Add water a little at a time and stir to achieve desired consistency.

Note: the sauce will thicken up in the fridge.
