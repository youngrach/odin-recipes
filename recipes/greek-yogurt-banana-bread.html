Greek Yogurt Banana Bread

Description

Adding Greek yogurt to your banana bread is a healthy way to add protein to banana bread while maintaining its moistness and flavor. Using whole wheat flour also bulks up this recipe with whole grains.

This recipe is inspired by Ambitious Kitchen's recipe for healthy banana bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 large bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 50 grams honey
  • 75 grams skyr
  • 40 grams avocado oil
  • 3/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 132 grams whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 4 grams baking soda
  • 58 grams toasted walnuts, roughly chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350ÂºF. Line an 8x8-in. square baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. Combine the bananas, eggs, honey, skyr, avocado oil, and vanilla extract in a large bowl. Mix well.
  3. Add the flour, cinnamon, salt, and baking soda and mix until just combined. Do NOT over-mix or the bread will fall flat.
  4. Fold in the chopped walnuts and pour into your baking pan.
  5. Bake for about 45 minutes, or until a chopstick inserted comes out with few crumbs attached.
